Professional Lawn Care Services in Westfield, NJ

Our Comprehensive Process

Our lawn care process begins with a thorough assessment of your property's unique needs, soil conditions, and grass type to create a customized maintenance plan. We start each season with essential services like dethatching and aeration to ensure proper water and nutrient absorption, followed by targeted fertilization using premium, environmentally-responsible products. Throughout the growing season, our certified technicians provide regular mowing at optimal heights, precise edging for clean lines, and strategic weed control treatments. We also monitor for common issues like grub infestations, fungal diseases, and drought stress, addressing problems before they compromise your lawn's health. This systematic approach ensures your grass receives exactly what it needs, when it needs it, for sustained beauty and resilience.

What's Included

Our complete lawn care package includes weekly mowing during peak season, professional edging and trimming, seasonal fertilization treatments, pre-emergent and post-emergent weed control, and organic soil amendments. You can expect consistent scheduling with the same trained crew, detailed service reports after each visit, and proactive communication about any issues we identify. Common signs you need professional lawn care include patchy or thinning grass, persistent weeds, yellowing or brown spots, compacted soil that doesn't absorb water properly, or simply not having time to maintain your yard consistently. Without proper maintenance, lawns quickly develop costly problems like bare spots that invite weeds, thatch buildup that prevents healthy growth, and pest infestations that can destroy entire sections of grass, ultimately requiring expensive renovation or complete re-sodding.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should my lawn be mowed during growing season?
Most Westfield lawns benefit from weekly mowing during peak growing season (late spring through early fall). We adjust frequency based on growth rate, weather conditions, and grass type to maintain optimal height without stressing your lawn.
What does lawn care service cost and when do you schedule treatments?
Our comprehensive lawn care packages start at $150 per month and vary based on property size and specific needs. We provide free estimates and schedule treatments based on seasonal requirements, typically beginning in early spring and continuing through late fall.
Will I need to be home during lawn care visits?
No, you don't need to be present during our visits - we work efficiently around your schedule. We'll provide advance notice of our arrival, complete detailed service reports, and communicate any important findings or recommendations via phone or email after each visit.
