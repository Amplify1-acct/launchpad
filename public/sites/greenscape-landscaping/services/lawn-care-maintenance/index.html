🌱 Lawn Care & Maintenance

Professional Lawn Care Services in Westfield, NJ

Your lawn is the foundation of your property's curb appeal, and maintaining its health and beauty requires consistent, professional care. At GreenScape Landscaping, we understand that Westfield homeowners take pride in their outdoor spaces and deserve lawns that reflect the quality of their neighborhoods. Our comprehensive lawn care and maintenance services are designed to keep your grass lush, healthy, and beautiful throughout every season. Trust our experienced team to transform your lawn into the envy of your street.

Our Complete Care Process

Our lawn care and maintenance process begins with a thorough assessment of your property's unique needs, including soil conditions, grass type, drainage patterns, and sun exposure. We then develop a customized treatment plan that includes regular mowing with sharp, professionally maintained equipment, precise edging along walkways and garden beds, and strategic fertilization based on seasonal requirements. Our team follows a consistent schedule that includes weekly or bi-weekly visits during growing season, ensuring your lawn receives the attention it needs when it needs it. Each visit includes a visual inspection for potential issues like pest problems, disease symptoms, or irrigation concerns, allowing us to address problems before they become costly repairs.

What's Included Always

Our comprehensive lawn care service includes professional mowing at the optimal height for your grass type, precise edging and trimming around all landscape features, debris removal, and seasonal fertilization programs tailored to New Jersey's climate. You can expect punctual, reliable service with clear communication about scheduling and any observations about your lawn's condition. Common signs you need professional lawn care include uneven growth patterns, brown or bare spots, excessive weeds, or simply lack of time to maintain consistent care. Without proper maintenance, lawns quickly develop thatch buildup, pest infestations, and disease problems that can cost thousands to remediate and may require complete lawn renovation.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often will you service my lawn?
We typically provide weekly service during peak growing season (spring through early fall) and bi-weekly service during slower growth periods. Our schedule is customized based on your lawn's specific needs and growth patterns.
What does lawn care service cost?
Pricing varies based on property size, service frequency, and specific treatments needed, typically ranging from $75-150 per visit. We provide free estimates and can create seasonal packages that offer better value than individual services.
Do I need to be home during service?
No, you don't need to be present during our visits, though we appreciate access to water and any specific instructions. We'll communicate about scheduling in advance and leave detailed notes about the work completed and any observations about your lawn's condition.
