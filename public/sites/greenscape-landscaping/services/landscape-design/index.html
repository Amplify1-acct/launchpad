Our Design Process

Our comprehensive landscape design process begins with an in-depth consultation at your Westfield property, where we assess your outdoor space, discuss your goals, and understand your budget and timeline. We carefully analyze your property's unique characteristics including soil conditions, drainage, sun exposure, and existing features to create a design that works harmoniously with your environment. Our talented designers then develop detailed plans and 3D renderings that showcase proposed plantings, hardscape elements, water features, and lighting solutions. We present multiple design options and work collaboratively with you to refine every detail until the plan perfectly matches your vision. Throughout the process, we consider maintenance requirements, seasonal interest, and long-term sustainability to ensure your landscape remains beautiful for years to come.

What's Included

Our landscape design service includes a comprehensive site analysis, custom design plans, detailed plant selections, hardscape recommendations, and professional 3D visualizations of your future outdoor space. You'll receive a complete plant list with mature sizes, care instructions, and seasonal characteristics, plus detailed layouts for patios, walkways, retaining walls, and other structural elements. Common signs you need professional design include an outdated landscape, poor drainage issues, lack of privacy, or simply wanting to maximize your outdoor living potential. Without proper design, homeowners often face costly mistakes like choosing inappropriate plants, poor layout decisions, drainage problems, and missed opportunities to create functional outdoor living spaces. Our detailed plans eliminate guesswork and ensure your investment delivers maximum impact and long-term satisfaction.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the landscape design process take?
Most landscape design projects take 2-3 weeks from initial consultation to final plan delivery. This timeline allows for thorough site analysis, creative development, and client revisions to ensure the final design meets all your expectations.
What does landscape design cost in Westfield?
Landscape design fees typically range from $1,500 to $5,000 depending on property size and project complexity. We provide transparent pricing upfront and often credit design fees toward installation when you choose us for the complete project.
Do you handle permits and installation coordination?
Yes, we manage all necessary permits for hardscape elements and coordinate with utility companies for any required clearances. Our team also provides seamless installation services, ensuring your design is executed exactly as planned with professional craftsmanship.
