HVAC Maintenance Plans for Scotch Plains Homes

Keep your heating and cooling system running efficiently year-round with ProComfort HVAC's comprehensive maintenance plans. Our local Scotch Plains team provides preventive care that extends equipment life, reduces energy costs, and prevents unexpected breakdowns. With over a decade serving Union County families, we understand the unique climate challenges your HVAC system faces. Regular maintenance isn't just smart - it's essential for comfort, safety, and savings.

Our Maintenance Process

Our certified technicians follow a detailed 21-point inspection checklist during each maintenance visit to your Scotch Plains home. We begin by examining all system components, including filters, coils, electrical connections, and moving parts for wear or damage. Next, we clean essential components, lubricate moving parts, test safety controls, and calibrate your thermostat for optimal performance. We also check refrigerant levels, inspect ductwork for leaks, and ensure proper airflow throughout your home. Finally, we provide you with a detailed report of our findings and recommendations, giving you complete transparency about your system's condition and any potential issues we've identified.

Why Choose ProComfort

Our maintenance plans include bi-annual tune-ups, priority emergency service, discounted repairs, and complimentary filter replacements throughout the year. You'll receive thorough cleaning of coils and components, belt adjustments, electrical connection tightening, and comprehensive system performance testing. Common signs your system needs maintenance include rising energy bills, uneven heating or cooling, strange noises, frequent cycling, or poor indoor air quality. Without regular maintenance, these minor issues can escalate into costly repairs, complete system failures, or dangerous safety hazards like carbon monoxide leaks. Our proactive approach catches problems early, saving you money and ensuring your family's comfort and safety.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should my HVAC system be maintained?
We recommend bi-annual maintenance - once in spring before cooling season and once in fall before heating season. This schedule ensures optimal performance year-round and helps identify potential issues before they become costly problems.
What does a maintenance plan cost and what payment options are available?
Our maintenance plans start at $189 annually and include two tune-ups plus additional benefits like priority service and repair discounts. We offer flexible payment options including monthly billing to spread the cost throughout the year.
What should I expect during a maintenance visit?
Each visit takes 60-90 minutes and includes a thorough 21-point inspection, cleaning, and testing of your system. We'll provide you with a detailed report of our findings and discuss any recommendations before leaving your home.
