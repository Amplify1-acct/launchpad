Our Maintenance Process

ProComfort HVAC's maintenance plans follow a systematic approach designed to catch potential issues before they become expensive problems. Our certified technicians perform thorough inspections of all system components, including filters, coils, electrical connections, and refrigerant levels. We clean essential components, lubricate moving parts, and test system performance to ensure optimal efficiency. Each visit includes a detailed report of findings and recommendations for any needed improvements. Our process is designed to maximize your system's lifespan while maintaining manufacturer warranties and keeping your energy bills as low as possible.

What's Included

Our comprehensive maintenance plans include bi-annual tune-ups, priority scheduling for repairs, and exclusive member discounts on parts and labor. Each service visit covers filter replacement, system cleaning, safety inspections, and performance testing to identify worn components before they fail. Signs you need professional maintenance include rising energy bills, uneven temperatures, strange noises, or reduced airflow throughout your home. Without regular maintenance, your HVAC system works harder, uses more energy, and is more likely to break down during extreme weather when you need it most. Our plans ensure your system receives the attention it needs to operate reliably and efficiently.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I have my HVAC system maintained?
We recommend bi-annual maintenance - once in spring for cooling season and once in fall for heating season. This schedule ensures your system is prepared for peak demand periods and helps identify issues before they cause breakdowns.
What does a maintenance plan cost and when do you schedule visits?
Our maintenance plans start at competitive rates with flexible payment options available. We schedule visits at your convenience and provide advance notice before each appointment, with plan members receiving priority scheduling for both maintenance and any needed repairs.
What can I expect during a maintenance visit?
Each visit includes a comprehensive system inspection, cleaning, filter replacement, and performance testing that typically takes 60-90 minutes. You'll receive a detailed report of our findings and any recommendations, plus we're always happy to answer questions about your system's operation.
