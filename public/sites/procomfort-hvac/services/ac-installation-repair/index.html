Expert AC Installation & Repair Scotch Plains

When your air conditioning system fails during New Jersey's sweltering summers, ProComfort HVAC is your trusted local partner for fast, reliable AC installation and repair services. Our certified technicians have been serving Scotch Plains families and businesses for years, ensuring your home stays cool and comfortable year-round. Whether you need emergency repairs or a complete system replacement, we deliver professional service you can count on.

Our AC Service Process

Our comprehensive AC installation and repair process begins with a thorough diagnostic assessment of your current system and cooling needs. Our certified technicians evaluate your home's size, insulation, ductwork, and existing equipment to determine the most efficient solution. For repairs, we quickly identify the root cause of the problem and explain all options before beginning work. New installations involve precise load calculations to ensure proper sizing, followed by professional installation that meets all local codes and manufacturer specifications. We test every system thoroughly before completion, ensuring optimal performance and energy efficiency for years to come.

What's Included

Every AC service includes a complete system evaluation, detailed written estimate, and warranty on all parts and labor. Our repair services cover common issues like refrigerant leaks, compressor failures, electrical problems, and thermostat malfunctions. Signs you need our services include warm air from vents, unusual noises, frequent cycling, high energy bills, or poor airflow. New installations include removal of old equipment, professional installation, system testing, and a comprehensive walkthrough of your new system's features. Ignoring AC problems can lead to complete system failure, costly emergency repairs, higher energy bills, and uncomfortable indoor temperatures during peak summer months.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does AC installation typically take?
Most residential AC installations are completed within 4-8 hours, depending on system complexity and any necessary modifications to existing ductwork. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your initial consultation and keep you informed throughout the process.
Do you offer emergency AC repair services?
Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency AC repair services throughout Scotch Plains and surrounding areas. Our emergency service includes evenings, weekends, and holidays, because we know AC failures don't wait for convenient times.
What should I expect during an AC service call?
