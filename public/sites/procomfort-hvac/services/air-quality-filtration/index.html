Our Comprehensive Assessment Process

Our air quality service begins with a thorough indoor air quality assessment using professional-grade testing equipment to measure pollutants, allergens, and contaminants in your home. We examine your existing HVAC system, ductwork, and current filtration setup to identify problem areas and potential sources of poor air quality. Our certified technicians then analyze humidity levels, dust accumulation, and airflow patterns throughout your Scotch Plains home. Based on these findings, we develop a customized air quality improvement plan that addresses your specific needs and budget. This comprehensive approach ensures we target the root causes of air quality issues rather than just treating symptoms.

Complete Filtration System Solutions

Our air quality service includes high-efficiency air filter installation, whole-home air purification systems, UV light sanitizers, and humidity control solutions tailored to your home's needs. You can expect improved air circulation, reduced allergens, elimination of odors, and cleaner air throughout every room in your Scotch Plains residence. Common signs you need our service include frequent allergies, excessive dust buildup, lingering odors, or family members experiencing respiratory issues at home. Frequently Asked Questions

How often should air filters be replaced?
Most standard air filters should be replaced every 1-3 months, depending on usage and home conditions. High-efficiency filters and whole-home air purification systems may have different maintenance schedules, which we'll explain during your consultation.
What does an air quality assessment cost?
Our comprehensive air quality assessments are competitively priced and include detailed testing and analysis of your home's air quality. We offer free estimates on filtration system installations, and many improvements qualify for energy efficiency rebates.
How long does installation take?
Most air filtration system installations are completed within 2-4 hours, depending on the complexity of your system. Whole-home air purification systems may require a full day, but we'll provide a detailed timeline during your consultation.
