Our Air Quality Process

Our comprehensive air quality assessment begins with a thorough inspection of your current HVAC system and indoor air conditions using professional-grade testing equipment. We measure particulate levels, humidity, and identify potential sources of contamination throughout your Scotch Plains home. Next, we analyze your existing filtration system's effectiveness and recommend upgrades tailored to your specific needs, whether that's high-efficiency particulate filters, UV air purifiers, or whole-home air cleaning systems. Our certified technicians then professionally install your chosen air quality solutions, ensuring seamless integration with your existing HVAC system. Finally, we provide detailed instructions on maintenance schedules and monitor system performance to guarantee optimal air purification results year-round.

What's Included Service

Our air quality and filtration service includes comprehensive system evaluation, professional-grade air quality testing, and installation of premium filtration equipment designed for New Jersey's climate conditions. You can expect detailed reporting on your home's current air quality levels, customized recommendations for improvement, and professional installation of HEPA filters, electronic air cleaners, or UV germicidal lights as needed. Common signs you need our service include persistent allergies, dusty surfaces despite regular cleaning, lingering odors, or family members experiencing respiratory irritation at home. If these air quality issues are ignored, they can lead to worsened health symptoms, reduced HVAC system efficiency, higher energy bills, and potentially costly system repairs due to contamination buildup.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I replace my air filters?
Standard filters should be replaced every 1-3 months depending on usage and home conditions. High-efficiency filters may last 6-12 months, and we'll provide a customized replacement schedule during your service visit.
What does air quality testing and filtration cost?
Air quality assessments start at $150, with filtration system installations ranging from $300-$2,500 depending on your home's needs. We provide detailed estimates after our initial evaluation and offer financing options for larger installations.
How long does installation take?
Most air filtration system installations are completed within 2-4 hours, depending on the complexity of your chosen system. Whole-home air purification systems may require a full day, and we'll schedule installation at your convenience with minimal disruption to your daily routine.
