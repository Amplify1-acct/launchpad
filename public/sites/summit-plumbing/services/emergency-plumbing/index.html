Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you respond to plumbing emergencies?
We typically arrive within 30-60 minutes of your call, depending on your location in Cranford and current service demand. Our trucks are strategically positioned throughout the area to ensure rapid response times. For true emergencies involving flooding or sewage, we prioritize these calls and often arrive even faster.
What are your emergency service rates and payment options?
Emergency service calls include a flat diagnostic fee that's applied toward any repair work we perform. We provide upfront pricing before beginning any work, so you'll know the total cost with no surprises. We accept cash, check, and all major credit cards for your convenience.
What should I do while waiting for your emergency technician?
First, locate and turn off your main water shut-off valve if there's active flooding or a major leak. Move valuables away from affected areas and take photos for insurance purposes if safe to do so. Our dispatcher will provide specific guidance based on your particular emergency situation when you call.
