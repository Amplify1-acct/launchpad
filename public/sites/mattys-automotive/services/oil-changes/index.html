🛢️ Oil Changes

Professional Oil Changes in Springfield, NJ

Keep your vehicle running smoothly with professional oil change services at Matty's Automotive in Springfield, NJ. Our experienced technicians use high-quality motor oil and filters to protect your engine and extend its lifespan. Regular oil changes are one of the most important maintenance services you can provide for your vehicle. Trust our local team to keep your car, truck, or SUV performing at its best.

Our Oil Change Process

At Matty's Automotive, our comprehensive oil change service begins with a thorough inspection of your current oil level and condition. We drain the old oil completely, replace your oil filter with a premium quality filter, and refill your engine with the manufacturer-recommended grade of fresh motor oil. Our certified technicians also perform a multi-point inspection during every oil change, checking fluid levels, belts, hoses, and battery condition to ensure your vehicle is running safely. We take the time to explain our findings and recommendations, so you're always informed about your vehicle's maintenance needs.

What's Included Service

Every oil change at Matty's Automotive includes up to 5 quarts of quality motor oil, a new oil filter, and a comprehensive multi-point inspection of your vehicle's key systems. We check transmission fluid, brake fluid, coolant levels, air filter condition, and tire pressure at no additional charge. Common signs you need an oil change include dark or gritty oil, engine noise, dashboard warning lights, or if it's been 3,000-5,000 miles since your last service. Neglecting regular oil changes can lead to engine damage, reduced fuel efficiency, and costly repairs that far exceed the cost of routine maintenance.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I get my oil changed?
Most vehicles need oil changes every 3,000 to 5,000 miles, depending on your driving conditions and oil type. We'll help you determine the right interval for your specific vehicle and driving habits.
How long does an oil change take?
Our typical oil change service takes 30-45 minutes to complete. We recommend calling ahead to schedule an appointment, but we also welcome walk-ins when possible.
Do you offer synthetic oil options?
Yes, we offer conventional, high-mileage, and full synthetic oil options to meet your vehicle's needs and budget. Our technicians can recommend the best oil type for your specific vehicle and driving conditions.
